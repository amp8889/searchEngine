Usage[edit]

This documentation sub-template is for transclusion in the "See also" sections of WikiLove templates. There are no parameters: 

=== See also ===
{{WikiLove see also}}
The above documentation is transcluded from Template:WikiLove see also/doc. (edit | history)
Editors can experiment in this template's sandbox (create | mirror) and testcases (create) pages.
