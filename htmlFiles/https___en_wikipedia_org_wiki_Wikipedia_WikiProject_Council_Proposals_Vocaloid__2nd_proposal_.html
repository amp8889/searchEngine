Description[edit]

Vocaloid is a very broad subject matter that, even though it is a niche subject probably needing a taskforce, has so many grounds to cover in terms of types of content that i dont even know where to put a taskforce. This project, moreso a "miniproject" of sorts, a taskforce not directly connected to one project, rather than the at least 3 this subject matter would need. I know this had a former proposal roughly 10 years ago, but with the right people I believe enough work can be done to update this specific side of culture on en wikipedia -Astral~(he/him/his) 02:25, 4 March 2024 (UTC)[reply]

Discussion[edit]

AstralAlley, welcome to Wikipedia! A WikiProject is a group of editors, not a subject area. Until you have already found a significant group of editors (usually six to 12 active editors) who want to work together, you should not create any pages for this.

I suggest that you instead try to WP:REVIVE Wikipedia:WikiProject Electronic music, as that is probably the central subject area, and there are a couple of editors interested in the broader subject who might be willing to work with you. That said, if you'd like to find a bigger group, then Wikipedia:WikiProject Video games is probably the most active group that will have an interest in supporting editors who are working in this area. Good luck, WhatamIdoing (talk) 01:59, 15 March 2024 (UTC)[reply]

(kagepro, although started as a song series, is a multimedia manga anime series as of now.). thnx 4 the tips though, i will def look into these options. -Astral~(he/him/his) 11:42, 15 March 2024 (UTC)[reply]
