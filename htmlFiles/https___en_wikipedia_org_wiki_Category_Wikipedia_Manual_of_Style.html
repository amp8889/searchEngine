From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
This is a maintenance category, used for maintenance of the Wikipedia project. It is not part of the encyclopedia and contains non-article pages, or groups articles by status rather than subject. Do not include this category in content categories.
Shortcut
  • CAT:MOS
Description: This category contains style guidelines about English Wikipedia. Guidelines are sets of best practices that are supported by consensus and should generally be followed.
The main page for this category is Wikipedia:Manual of Style.
