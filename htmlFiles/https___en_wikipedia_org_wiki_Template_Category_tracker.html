Template documentation[view] [edit] [history] [purge]

This template aims to track various maintenance backlogs across Wikipedia. It is split up into several categories:

  • Template:Category tracker – everything
  • Template:Category tracker/Immediate – immediate requests such as speedy deletions
  • Template:Category tracker/Deletion – non-speedy deletion processes
  • Template:Category tracker/Images – image copyright and non-free use problems
  • Template:Category tracker/Protection – protected page counts
  • Template:Category tracker/Cleanup – article cleanup
  • Template:Category tracker/Miscellaneous – everything else

Please note that some of the counts may be inaccurate. If a count is inaccurate, try purging the relevant template. Some cleanup types don't have tracking categories, an example is Category:Wikipedia neutral point of view disputes. The database index may also be out of date due to prior bugs, see bug 18488.

These templates use expensive parser functions. Using this template on pages with lots of these may cause something to break.

The above documentation is transcluded from Template:Category tracker/doc. (edit | history)
Editors can experiment in this template's sandbox (create | mirror) and testcases (create) pages.
