The main article for this category is Roman Empire.

This category refers to the period of Roman history known as the Roman Empire (as opposed to Category:Roman Kingdom and Category:Roman Republic); it covers the centuries from the late 1st century BC, to the Fall of Constantinople in 1453 AD.

For topics not found here - because they overlap with the Roman Republic - e.g. economy, geography and law: see Category:Ancient Rome.

For the Eastern Roman Empire, see Category:Byzantine Empire.
