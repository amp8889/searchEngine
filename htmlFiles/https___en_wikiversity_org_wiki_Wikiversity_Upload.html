Wikimedia Commons acts as a common repository of freely-licensed educational media content (images, sound and video clips) for the various projects of the Wikimedia Foundation. Any content uploaded to Commons will be immediately available for use on Wikiversity and its sister projects.

Whilst Commons only accepts freely-licensed content, limited non-free content can be uploaded locally for use on Wikiversity. Please select the source of the content you wish to upload and you will be directed to the appropriate upload form.

Where is the work from? (Click on the appropriate link)

  • It is entirely my own work
  • It is someone else's work from Flickr (more information on uploading Flickr images)
  • It is a derivative work of a file from Commons
  • It is from another Wikimedia project (Wikipedia, Wikibooks, Wikinews, etc.)
  • It is from a US federal government source
  • It is a free software screenshot
  • It is from somewhere else
  • It is a non-free fair use image (Non-free logo, cover, screenshot, artwork etc.)
  • I don't know who the author is, or I don't know what license applies
  • I need help figuring out what the license is
