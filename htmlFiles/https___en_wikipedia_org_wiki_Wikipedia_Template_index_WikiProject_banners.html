For the non-WikiProject talk page template index, see Wikipedia:Template index/Talk namespace.

This template index is for WikiProject banners that are used on various talk pages. Typically, they would be placed at the top of talk pages of articles or pages covered by the associated WikiProject.

Instructions on how to create WikiProject banners can be found at {{WPBannerMeta}} and Wikipedia:WikiProject Council/Guide/Technical notes § Advanced project banners.
