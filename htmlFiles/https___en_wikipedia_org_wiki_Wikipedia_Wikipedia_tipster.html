From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Shortcut
  • WP:TIPSTER

A Wikipedia tipster is a Wikipedian who has written at least one tip of the day for Wikipedia's tip of the day project. The main benefit of being a Wikipedia tipster, aside from helping others with useful tips on how to use Wikipedia, is that you get to display one of our fun userboxes on your user page.
