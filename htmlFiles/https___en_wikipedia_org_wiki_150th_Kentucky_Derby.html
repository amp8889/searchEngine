The 2024 Kentucky Derby (officially, the 150th Running of the Kentucky Derby Presented by Woodford Reserve) will be the 150th running of the Kentucky Derby. It will take place on May 4, 2024, at Churchill Downs, in Louisville, Kentucky.[1]
