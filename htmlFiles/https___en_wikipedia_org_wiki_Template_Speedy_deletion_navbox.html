Template documentation[view] [edit] [history] [purge]

This template is a navigation aid intended as a comprehensive list of everything relating to speedy deletion on the English Wikipedia.

Usage

The template usually goes at the bottom of the page, before the categories and interwiki links. (See Wikipedia:Layout.) To add it to a page, use the following syntax:

{{speedy deletion navbox}}

See also

  • {{Speedy deletion templates}}
  • {{Speedy deletion notices}}
The above documentation is transcluded from Template:Speedy deletion navbox/doc. (edit | history)
Editors can experiment in this template's sandbox (edit | diff) and testcases (create) pages.
