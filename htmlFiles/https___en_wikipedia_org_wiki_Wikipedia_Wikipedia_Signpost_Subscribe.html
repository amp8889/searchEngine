Subscribing to The Signpost

There are several different ways to keep up with the latest Signpost issues:

For English Wikipedia users

Talk page delivery

The most direct way to subscribe to The Signpost, if you have an account on the Wikimedia projects, is log in to it and add your name to our list; we will post the Post to your user talk page. To subscribe or unsubscribe, add or remove your name from this list.

Template subscription

You can subscribe via template, by placing a {{Signpost-subscription}}, {{User Signpost-subscription}}, or {{Signpost-subscription-inline}} template anywhere where you want to keep up with Signpost publications (normally on your user page).

Watchlist notification

Click here to get a notification for each issue on your English Wikipedia watchlist via our current-issue link.

Global delivery on Wikimedia projects

The list mentioned above supports user talk deliveries on our home wiki, the English Wikipedia. To get monthly Signpost deliveries to your user-talk page at another Wikimedia project, use our global delivery list on the Meta Wiki.

E-mail

You can subscribe to e-mails for new Signpost issues by typing your e-mail address here. The mailing list is WikimediaAnnounce-l, which is fairly low-traffic, so you will get about 50% Signpost emails and 50% other stuff. We also email the Wikimedia-L list, which gets a great deal more traffic.

RSS feeds

You can subscribe to Signpost RSS feeds (using a feed aggregator). Feeds are available for:

  • Recent Signpost stories (one item per story)
  • Recent single-page editions (one item per publication)

Social media

Follow The Signpost on these social media sites to get notices of new issues, as well as occasionally links to significant stories as they break ahead of publication:

  • Twitter: @wikisignpost
  • Mastodon: @WikiSignpost@wikis.world
  • Facebook: wikisignpost

Note: To have the Signpost delivered to your talk page, add your user talk page (User talk:Example). You can also add a subpage if you prefer (User talk:Example/Signpost), but you will need to put it on your watchlist in order to be notified of updates.
