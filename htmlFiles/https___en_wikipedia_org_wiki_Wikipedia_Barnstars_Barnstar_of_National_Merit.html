A Barnstar of National Merit may be awarded to an editor who contributes significantly by expanding or improving Wikipedia's coverage of any past or present continent or regional grouping, country, or subnational place such as a province or city.

For alternative images, see "Barnstar of National Merit 2.0" and commons:Category:Barnstars of National Merit.

General
{{subst:Barnstar of National Merit|message ~~~~|alt}} Geography The Barnstar of National Merit may be awarded to an editor who contributes significantly by expanding or improving Wikipedia's coverage of any particular country. This barnstar was proposed by Redux on 23 February 2005 and designed by Zscout370.
Continents and regional groupings
{{subst:The AfricaProject Award|message ~~~~}} Category:Africa The Africa Award may be awarded to those who have done exemplary work on Africa-related articles. This award was introduced by the Wikipedia:Africa-related regional notice board at the end of 2005.
{{subst:The Barnstar of European Merit|message ~~~~}} Category:European Union
Category:Europe 		The Barnstar of European Merit may be awarded to those who have contributed tirelessly with excellent edits in articles related to the European Union or Europe in general. It is not limited to members of WP:European Union or WP:Europe. It was introduced by Chaza93 on 30 May 2007 and redesigned on 31 May 2007 by Ssolbergj.
{{subst:NATO barnstar|message ~~~~}} Category:NATO The NATO barnstar is awarded to users who have made extraordinary contributions to NATO organizational and military history-related articles.
{{subst:Commonwealth Barnstar|message ~~~~}} Category:Commonwealth of Nations The Commonwealth Barnstar of Merit may be awarded to editors who've made significant contributions to Commonwealth of Nations-related articles. Proposed by Strike Eagle on 18 May 2012, designed by Penyulap on 19 May 2012 and completed by Thorpewilliam on 12 March 2022.
Countries
{{subst:The Albanian Barnstar of National Merit|message ~~~~}} Category:Albania The Albanian Barnstar of National Merit is awarded to users who have made extraordinary contributions to Albania-related articles, especially participants of the Albania WikiProject. It was introduced by Euriditi using an image uploaded by Cradel.
{{subst:The Australian Barnstar of National Merit|message ~~~~}} Category:Australia The Australian Barnstar of National Merit is awarded to users who have made extraordinary contributions to Australia-related articles, especially participants of the Australia WikiProject. It was introduced by G1ggy using an image uploaded by Chris 73.
{{subst:Bangladesh Barnstar|message ~~~~}} Category:Bangladesh The Bangladesh Barnstar is awarded to users who have made extraordinary contributions to Bangladesh-related articles, especially participants of the Bangladesh WikiProject. It was introduced by Rossi101 using an image uploaded by Rossi101.
{{subst:The Maple Leaf Award|type= |message ~~~~}} Category:Canada The Maple Leaf Award is for coverage of topics related to Canada, as well as for community work related to Canadian projects. It comes in Red (), Silver () and Gold () flavours. This award was created/introduced by Qyd in November 2007.
{{subst:WikiProject Colombia Award|message ~~~~}} Category:Colombia The WikiProject Colombia Award may be awarded to Wikipedians who show exceptional effort and dedication to articles related to WikiProject Colombia. The award was created by F3rn4nd0 on 24 September 2007.
{{subst:People's Republic of China National Merit Medal|message ~~~~}} Category:China The People's Republic of China National Merit Medal may be awarded to Wikipedians who show exceptional effort and dedication to articles related to WikiProject China. The award was created by hongdx on October 16, 2010.
{{subst:BoNM-Denmark|message ~~~~}} Category:Denmark The Danish Barnstar of National Merit is an award that you can give to users you think deserve it based on their contributions to reach the goals of this project

{{subst:Mexican Barnstar of National Merit|message ~~~~}} Category:Mexico The Mexican Barnstar of National Merit may be awarded to Wikipedians who exhibit exceptional effort and dedication to articles related to Mexico. The award was created by Alex Covarrubias on 19 July 2010.

{{subst:The New Zealand Barnstar of National Merit|message ~~~~}} Category:New Zealand The New Zealand Barnstar of National Merit is awarded to users who have made significant contributions to New Zealand-related articles, particularly participants of the New Zealand WikiProject. It was created by Taifarious1.
{{subst:The Pakistan Star|message ~~~~}} Category:Pakistan The Pakistan Star is given to Wikipedians who work tirelessly and making outstanding contributions to WikiProject Pakistan.
{{subst:WikiProject Peru Award|message ~~~~}} Category:Peru The WikiProject Peru Award may be awarded to Wikipedians who exhibit exceptional effort and dedication to articles related to WikiProject Peru. The award was created by EVula on 28 May 2007.
{{subst:The Philippine Barnstar|message ~~~~}} Category:Philippines The Philippine Barnstar is the award given to Wikipedians recognized for their tireless efforts to improve and to further develop Philippines-related articles under the scope of the Wikipedia:Tambayan Philippines, formerly WP:PINOY. It was developed by TheCoffee on 9 September 2005.
{{subst:The Polish Barnstar of National Merit 2|message ~~~~}} Category:Poland The Polish Barnstar of National Merit is for articles about Poland.
{{subst:Romania Barnstar of National Merit|message ~~~~}} Category:Romania The Romania Barnstar of National Merit is awarded to users who have made extraordinary contributions to Romania and Latinity-related articles. The award was created by Gaston200 on 26 October 2008.
{{subst:South African Barnstar of National Merit|message ~~~~}} Category:South Africa The South African Barnstar of National Merit is awarded to users who have made extraordinary contributions to South African-related articles. The award was created by NJR_ZA on 7 November 2010.
{{subst:The Spain Barnstar of National Merit|message ~~~~}} Category:Spain The Spanish Barnstar of National Merit is awarded to users who have made extraordinary contributions to Spanish-related articles.
{{subst:The Sri Lankan Barnstar of National Merit|message ~~~~}} Category:Sri Lanka The Sri Lankan Barnstar of National Merit is awarded to users who have made extraordinary contributions to Sri Lanka-related articles. It is not limited to members of Wikipedia:WikiProject Sri Lanka. It was introduced by Chanakal.
{{subst:Ukraine Barnstar|message ~~~~}} Category:Ukraine The Ukraine Barnstar is to be awarded to a user who has made outstanding contributions to WikiProject Ukraine. This award was introduced by Chaza93 on 8 August 2007.
{{subst:The Oʻzbekiston qahramoni Barnstar|message ~~~~}} Category:Uzbekistan The Oʻzbekiston Qahramoni is awarded to users who have made extraordinary contributions creating and adding to articles on the history, culture, infustructure, and notable people of Uzbekistan. It was introduced by PlanespotterA320.
Subnational places, including provinces and cities
{{subst:Devon Barnstar|message ~~~~}} Category:Devon The Devon Barnstar of Merit may be awarded to Wikipedians who exhibit exceptional effort and dedication to articles related to WikiProject Devon.
{{subst:The Galician Barnstar of National Merit|message ~~~~}} Category:Galicia The Galician Barnstar of Merit may be awarded to Wikipedians who exhibit exceptional effort and dedication to articles related to Galicia.
{{subst:Louisiana Barnstar|message ~~~~}} Category:Louisiana The Pelican in Her Piety Barnstar is given to recognize editors who have improved the coverage of Louisiana on Wikipedia. It was introduced by Carter (Tcr25).
{{subst:The Minnesota barnstar|message ~~~~}} Category:Minnesota The Minnesota Barnstar is awarded to users who have made extraordinary contributions to Minnesota-related articles. It was introduced by Paul2520.
{{subst:Puerto Rico National Merit Medal|message ~~~~}} Category:Puerto Rico The Puerto Rico National Merit Medal is to be awarded to dedicated users whose contributions and dedication to articles regarding Puerto Rico are above and beyond from what is normally expected. Generally, the recipient should have at least one featured article about Puerto Rico or has created a significant amount of Puerto Rico related articles. It was introduced by Tony the Marine.
Historical places
{{subst:Wikiproject Assyria Barnstar of National Merit|message ~~~~}} Category:Assyria The WikiProject Assyria Barnstar is to be awarded to a user who has demonstrated great effort and dedication at WikiProject Assyria. This award was created by SchnitzelMannGreek and was designated as the official award of WikiProject Assyria on 4 August 2009.
{{subst:The Yugoslavia Barnstar of National Merit|message ~~~~}} Category:Yugoslavia The Yugoslavia Barnstar of National Merit may be awarded to Wikipedians who exhibit exceptional effort and dedication to articles related to WikiProject Yugoslavia, formerly WikiProject Former Yugoslavia. The award was created by Gaston200 on 8 October 2008.
{{subst:Soviet Union Barnstar of National Merit|message ~~~~}} Category:Soviet Union The Soviet Union Barnstar of National Merit may be awarded to Wikipedians who exhibit exceptional effort and dedication to articles related to WikiProject Soviet Union, especially those who go above and beyond to write about subjects that underwent undue political censorship (ie, industrial disasters, biographies of dissidents, etc).
Other
