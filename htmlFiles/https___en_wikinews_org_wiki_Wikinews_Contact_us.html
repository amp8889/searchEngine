How to contact Wikinews

Wikinews, a wiki-based open content news site, is operated by volunteers. Though it provides a free service without advertisements, it is a charitable endeavour and privately funded. It has no "editorial board", and its entries are written by volunteers around the world. Please see Wikinews:About for more information.

  • Contact the Wikinews mailing list at wikinews-l AT lists.wikimedia.org (Your email will be held for review if you are not subscribed to the list)
  • Contact for any issues that need special attention- wikinews AT Wikimedia DOT org
