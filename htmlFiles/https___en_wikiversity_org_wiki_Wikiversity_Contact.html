Wikiversity is a project of the Wikimedia Foundation. To contact the Wikimedia Foundation, please refer to their Contact webpage.

Contacting users on Wikiversity[edit source]

There are a number of ways you can contact users on Wikiversity. You may

  • Post to a particular community member's talk page;
  • Post to a general interest page with a larger audience, such as the Colloquium
  • Chat with Wikiversity users;
  • Contact the support staff amongst the Wikiversity community; or
  • Write to the mailing list: Subscribe to Wikiversity-l and browse the archives.
