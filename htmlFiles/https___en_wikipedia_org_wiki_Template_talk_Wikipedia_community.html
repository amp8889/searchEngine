Wikipedia holidays, pomp and circumstance and all that jazz[edit]

Shall Wikipedia:Wikipedia holidays be added to 'General community topics' as Holidays? I'd included it but was reverted with a comment that nobody celebrates them, which is incorrect at least as to Wikipedia Day. The other four in-house holidays remember the honorees achievements, and while adding only 'Wikipedia Day' to the template seems a reasonable option it would be adequately covered under the entry 'Holidays' which includes much more. Thanks. Randy Kryn (talk) 11:12, 8 December 2021 (UTC)[reply]

I still do not agree. These are things made up by Jimmy Wales, they are not from the community and are not widely observed. Beeblebrox (talk) 03:51, 16 December 2021 (UTC)[reply]
Wales makes up some pretty good things. But at a minimum Wikipedia Day, not created by Wales, seems a pertinent addition, and has been well celebrated. Randy Kryn (talk) 04:07, 16 December 2021 (UTC)[reply]
