The World 100,000 Challenge

63.061%

The quest to bring about 100,000 article improvements and creations globally through a series of 50,000/10,000/1000 Challenges for different regions and countries. This is a loose challenge, a list will not be maintained here, but the components will function independently on each of the subpages. A rough percentage bar indicator of overall progress will be updated every few months.

Challenge components[edit]

As of 3 March 2024 we have 63,061 articles combined.
  • The 10,000 Challenge (UK and Ireland) (10,070)
  • The 50,000 Challenge (United States) (9,047)
    • The 2,000 Challenge (Pacific Northwest) (1,618)
    • The 10,000 Challenge (Northeast) (369)
    • The 2000 Challenge (California and Nevada) (198)
  • The 10,000 Challenge (Europe) (7,979)
    • The 2500 Challenge (Nordic) (1,669)
    • The 1000 Challenge (Turkey) (1,251)
  • The 10,000 Challenge (Asia) (7,013)
  • The 10,000 Challenge (Africa) (5,890)
  • The 10,000 Challenge (Canada) (5,132)
  • The 10,000 Challenge (Oceania) (3,730)
    • The 5000 Challenge (Australia) (3,650)
  • The 10,000 Challenge (Latin America) (2,342)
  • The 10,000 Challenge (India) (2,103)
  • The 1000 Challenge (Video games) (1,000)

Contests held to date[edit]

  • Awaken the Dragon (April 2016) - 1065 articles
  • The West Country Challenge (August 2016) - 519 articles
  • The Africa Destubathon (October-Nov 2016) - 2041 articles
  • The Women in Red World Contest (November 2017) - 2859 articles
  • The Months of African Cinema (October - November 2018)
  • The Great Britain and Ireland Destubathon (March 2020)
