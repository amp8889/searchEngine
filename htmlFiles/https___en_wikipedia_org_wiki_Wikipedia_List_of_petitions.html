From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

From time to time, Wikipedians publish petitions in the Wikipedia namespace, and request others to sign them. Remember that petitions should be avoided, so please try to think of alternative methods to accomplish whatever goal you have in mind before starting a new one.
