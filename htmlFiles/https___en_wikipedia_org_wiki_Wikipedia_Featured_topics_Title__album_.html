< Wikipedia:Featured topics

Title is the debut major-label studio album by the American singer-songwriter Meghan Trainor. Released on January 9, 2015, it replaced her 2014 extended play on the iTunes Store. Four successful singles were released from the album; "All About That Bass", "Lips Are Movin", "Dear Future Husband" and "Like I'm Gonna Lose You". Trainor embarked on That Bass Tour and the MTrain Tour in support of the album.
