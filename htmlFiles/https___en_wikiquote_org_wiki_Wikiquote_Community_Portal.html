Goings-on

#SheSaid

  • The #SheSaid campaign is a Wiki Loves Women initiative to celebrate the world's leading women. The project aims to increase the voice of women through the creation of new entries in Wikiquote or the improvement of existing ones.

Policy being drafted

  • Wikiquote:Quotability: Aiming to a core of Wikiquote inclusion policy.
  • Wikiquote:Civility: User behavior guideline. Now discussed on its talk page.
  • Wikiquote:Marking problematic pages is an introduction to the various tags which can be used to identify problems with pages, still in editing.
  • User:Aphaia/User page draft is a draft for user-page policy.
