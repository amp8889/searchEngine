Welcome to WikiProject Democracy! This project aims at improving and maintaining the democratic structures within the Wikipedia movement by:

  • making them more visible,
  • making participation in voting, elections, and centralized discussion easier by providing and spreading links,
  • and building a forum and community of contributors who like the idea of democratic wiki collaboration and cooperation.

You are very welcome to participate!
